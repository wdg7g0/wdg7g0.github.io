Your Journey

Informatiom on how to get here.

Come by bus

The VR park is located 500m away from St. James station, in the city center. Most buses in the red line will stop there. After you get to the station, exit through John St. and go straight for one block, the vr park will be located on your left.

Come by car

We have free parking. The entrance to the parking is located in the John St. number 400, in the city center. Don't forget to stamp your parking ticket with one of our collaborators.
